White matter pathology in the corpus callosum. a At 8 M post-TBI, histopathological analyses were performed on the corpus callosum at the level of the dorsal hippocampus, using Iba1 (microglia), NeuroSilver (axonal injury), SMI312A (neurofilament) and GFAP (astrocytes). Representative images for each stain are shown. (b) Quantification of (a) is plotted by reporting the density and size of microglia, the stain area of NeuroSilver, the density of neurofilament-positive axonal bulbs, and the GFAP immunofluorescence intensity of astrocytes. Scale bar represents 200 μm. Data are plotted as mean ± SE. (PDF 1515 kb)
